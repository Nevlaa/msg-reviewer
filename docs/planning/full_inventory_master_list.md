# SNAP Food Inventory Master List (SOP Dropdowns)

This list represents the full set of variety options available in the SNAP Store Survey form, categorized by the USDA staple food groups.

## 1. BREADS, GRAINS, OR CEREALS
**Flours & Grains:**
- Almond flour, Amaranth, Barley, Bean Flour, Buckwheat, Cassava Flour, Millet, Oats - Other, Pea Flour, Plantain Flour, Potato flour, Quinoa, Rice, Rice Flour, Rye, Sorghum, Tapioca, Teff flour, Wheat Flour/ Processed Foods, Yam flour.

**Breads & Baked Goods:**
- Bagels, Baking Mix (Corn Bread or Wheat Based), Bread/Loaf Bread, Buns/Rolls, Pitas/Naan, Tortillas (Corn or Flour).

**Cereals:**
- Cold Cereals (Corn, Rice, Wheat, Oat), Hot Cereals (Oatmeal, Rice, Wheat, Grits), Infant Cereals (Corn, Oat, Rice, Wheat).

**Pasta:**
- Pasta/Ramen (Wheat), Rice Noodles.

## 2. MEATS, POULTRY, FISH
**Land Animals:**
- Beef/Veal, Bison, Camel, Chicken, Cornish Hens, Duck, Elk, Goat, Goose, Kangaroo, Lamb/Mutton, Pork, Quail, Rabbit, Turkey, Venison.

**Seafood:**
- Abalone, Alligator, Anchovies, Arctic Char, Bream, Catfish, Caviar, Clams, Cod, Conch, Crab, Crawfish, Croaker, Eel, Flounder, Fluke Fish, Frog, Grouper, Haddock, Halibut, Herring, Lobster, Mackerel, Mahi, Mussels, Octopus, Oysters, Perch, Pollock, Salmon, Sardines, Scallops, Sea Bass, Shark, Shrimp, Snails, Snapper, Sole, Squid, Striped Bass, Swai, Swordfish, Tilapia, Trout, Tuna, Unidentified fish variety, Whiting.

**Eggs:**
- Chicken Eggs, Duck Eggs, Quail Eggs.

## 3. DAIRY / DAIRY SUBSTITUTE PRODUCTS
**Dairy:**
- Butter, Cheese (Dairy Based), Ghee, Milk (Dairy), Sour Cream, Yogurt (Dairy Based).

**Plant-Based Alternatives:**
- Almond Based Cheese, Almond Milk, Cashew milk, Coconut Milk, Hemp milk, Macadamia milk, Oat milk, Other Nut Milk, Rice Based Yogurt, Rice Milk, Soy Based Cheese, Soy Based Yogurt, Soy Milk, Yogurts (Coconut Based).

**Other:**
- Dairy-based nutrition bars, Infant Formula (Dairy, Soy, or Other), Margarine.

## 5. Variety Identification Examples
Variety is generally defined by the product kind or main ingredient.

### Vegetables or Fruits
*   **Potatoes** (e.g., fresh potatoes, frozen tater tots)
*   **Oranges** (e.g., 100% orange juice, fresh oranges)
*   **Tomatoes** (e.g., canned tomato soup, sun-dried tomatoes)
*   **Apples** (e.g., dried apples, pre-cut apple go-packs)
*   **Pumpkin** (e.g., canned pumpkin, fresh whole pumpkin)
*   **Bananas** (e.g., fresh bananas, frozen bananas)
*   **Lettuce** (e.g., fresh head of iceberg, bagged romaine)
*   **Pineapples** (e.g., canned pineapple rings, fresh whole pineapple)

### Meat, Poultry, or Fish
*   **Turkey** (e.g., deli-sliced, ground turkey)
*   **Chicken** (e.g., fresh cutlets, frozen nuggets)
*   **Beef** (e.g., ground beef, beef jerky)
*   **Tuna** (e.g., fresh steak, canned tuna)
*   **Catfish** (e.g., frozen filets, smoked packaged catfish)
*   **Lamb/Mutton** (e.g., fresh chops, ground lamb)
*   **Pork** (e.g., loin, fresh sliced ham)
*   **Chicken Eggs** (e.g., fresh eggs, liquid egg whites)

### Dairy Products
*   **Cheese** (e.g., cheddar slices, grated parmesan)
*   **Milk** (e.g., skim, whole milk)
*   **Almond-based milk** (e.g., refrigerated or shelf-stable)
*   **Butter** (e.g., salted or frozen sweet cream)
*   **Butter substitute** (e.g., margarine, non-dairy spread)
*   **Sour cream** (e.g., lite, organic)
*   **Yogurt** (e.g., French vanilla, nonfat peach)
*   **Infant formula** (e.g., liquid ready-to-feed, powdered milk formula)
*   **Soy infant formula** (e.g., liquid or powdered soy formula)

### Breads or Cereals
*   **Bread** (e.g., rye, multigrain)
*   **Pasta** (e.g., gluten-free spaghetti, whole wheat rotini)
*   **Tortillas** (e.g., corn, flour)
*   **Bagels** (e.g., poppy seed, plain)
*   **Pitas** (e.g., low-carb, whole wheat)
*   **Cold breakfast cereal** (e.g., rice-based, oat-based)
*   **Buns/rolls** (e.g., frozen dinner rolls, hot dog buns)
*   **Infant cereal** (e.g., wheat-based, oat-based)
*   **Rice** (e.g., bagged rice, rice-based frozen meals)
